## The OBXToken: A Deflationary Digital Asset

### What is OBXToken?

OBXToken is a specially designed cryptocurrency with a unique feature: **programmed scarcity**. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies that have unlimited supply, OBXToken is designed to become increasingly rare over time.

### Key Facts About OBXToken

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Total Initial Supply** | 100,000,000 OBX tokens |
| **Token Standard** | ERC-20 / BEP-20 (Blockchain compatible) |
| **Blockchain Networks** | BSC Mainnet, Ethereum, Polygon, and more |
| **Decimals** | 18 (like traditional money cents) |
| **Launch Date** | As announced by the team |

### How Scarcity Works: The Burn Mechanism

Every time someone transfers OBXToken, a small amount is automatically **burned** (removed from circulation permanently). This creates natural scarcity:

**The Burn Process:**
- Each transfer automatically removes **0.05%** of the transaction amount
- Example: If you send 1,000 OBX, 0.5 OBX is burned, 999.5 OBX reaches the recipient
- All burns are **permanent** and **publicly visible** on the blockchain

**The Burn Floor:**
- Burning stops automatically when supply reaches **41,000,000 OBX**
- This means **59,000,000 OBX** (59% of the original supply) will eventually be burned
- After the floor is reached, all transfers are **fee-free forever**

### Why Is This Important?

**For Holders:**
- Your tokens become more scarce over time
- Reduced supply typically increases value
- You benefit from the network's growth without buying more

**For The Community:**
- Prevents inflation
- Creates economic incentive for participation
- Rewards long-term holders

**For Transparency:**
- All burns are recorded on the blockchain
- Anyone can verify the total burned amount
- No hidden mechanisms or surprise changes

### Staking: Earn Rewards

Staking is how the community earns returns on their OBX holdings.

**What is Staking?**

Staking means locking your OBX tokens for a specific period in exchange for rewards. It's like a bank savings account that pays interest, except the rates and terms are controlled by the blockchain.

**How Staking Works:**

1. **Lock Period**: You choose how long to lock tokens (30, 90, 180, or 365 days)
2. **APY Rate**: Different periods have different annual return rates
3. **Automatic Rewards**: Interest accumulates daily
4. **Early Unlock**: Available with a small fee if needed
5. **Compounding**: Rewards can be restaked for compounding

**Staking Tiers:**

| Lock Period | Annual Return | Minimum | Maximum |
|-------------|---------------|---------|---------|
| 30 days | 12% APY | 100 OBX | Unlimited |
| 90 days | 18% APY | 500 OBX | Unlimited |
| 180 days | 24% APY | 1,000 OBX | Unlimited |
| 365 days | 36% APY | 5,000 OBX | Unlimited |

**Example Return:**
- **Stake**: 10,000 OBX for 365 days at 36% APY
- **Annual Reward**: 3,600 OBX
- **Monthly Average**: 300 OBX
- **After 1 Year**: 13,600 OBX (if not compounded)

**To Stake Your OBX:**

1. Go to "Staking" section
2. Click "New Stake"
3. Select your lock period
4. Enter amount to stake
5. Review APY and total rewards
6. Confirm transaction
7. Stake begins immediately

**Important Notes:**
- Staked tokens cannot be transferred during lock period
- You can create multiple stakes with different periods
- Rewards are credited automatically
- No actions needed after staking

## Team Wallet & On-Chain Transfers

### What is the Team Wallet?

The **Team Wallet** is a special wallet type designed for organizations and teams that need to:

- Manage large token balances collectively
- Execute team-approved transactions
- Track on-chain movements transparently
- Maintain audit trails for compliance

### Team Wallet Features

**On-Chain Processing:**
- All withdrawals execute directly on the blockchain
- Transactions are publicly visible on BSCScan
- No additional fees beyond the 0.05% token burn
- Faster settlement for large transfers

**Security & Governance:**
- Multiple approval layers for withdrawals
- Audit logs for all team actions
- Role-based access controls
- Real-time notifications for all movements

**Transparency:**
- Team members see the wallet address
- Anyone can verify transactions publicly
- Monthly reconciliation reports available
- Clear balance tracking

### Understanding Token Burn on Team Wallets

When a Team Wallet withdraws OBX:

**The Process:**
1. Team requests withdrawal of (e.g.) 1,000 OBX
2. Transfer initiated on blockchain
3. 0.05% burn calculated (0.5 OBX)
4. Recipient receives 999.5 OBX
5. 0.5 OBX burned permanently
6. Transaction recorded on BSCScan

**Why This Matters:**
- Burn creates scarcity for all holders
- Team benefits from this scarcity just like individual holders
- Aligns incentives: team success = token value appreciation
- Transparent on-chain audit trail

### Custody Model: Preserving Holder Count

A key principle of OBXCoin's Team Wallet design is **holder preservation**:

**What This Means:**
- Team Wallet doesn't automatically sweep user deposits to a central account
- User deposits remain in user wallets
- This prevents artificial reduction in holder count
- Each user maintains their position in the ecosystem

**Example:**
- User deposits 100 OBX to earn interest
- Tokens remain in user's wallet
- User is still counted as an OBX holder
- Community metrics show accurate holder numbers
- User maintains full control of their assets

This approach ensures that:
✓ Health metrics accurately reflect community size  
✓ Users maintain control of their assets  
✓ Community feels ownership of their tokens  
✓ System grows organically, not artificially

## Security & Safety

### Protecting Your Account

Your security is our top priority. Follow these essential practices:

**Password Security:**
- Create a strong password: 12+ characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols
- Don't reuse passwords across websites
- Update password every 6 months
- Never write down your password

**Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):**
- **Enable it immediately** on account creation
- Use authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
- Don't screenshot or photograph the backup codes
- Store backup codes in secure location (safe, password manager)
- Keep your phone secure (2FA app is installed there)

**Phishing Prevention:**
- ✗ Never click links in unsolicited emails
- ✗ Always type URL directly or use bookmarks
- ✗ Never enter password on pop-up windows
- ✗ Ignore requests for private keys or passwords
- ✓ Verify official support accounts
- ✓ Look for "https://" and lock icon
- ✓ Check email address carefully (look for slight misspellings)

### Wallet Security Best Practices

**Private Keys:**
- Your private key is sacred – guard it with your life
- Never share your private key with ANYONE
- Even OBXCoin team will never ask for it
- A compromised private key means loss of funds

**Transaction Safety:**
- Always verify recipient address three times
- Start with small test amounts to new addresses
- Keep transaction receipts for tax purposes
- Be suspicious of unsolicited offers

**Account Recovery:**
- Save your 2FA backup codes in multiple secure locations
- Consider printing them and storing in a safe
- Update recovery email if you change emails
- Never leave account compromised – report immediately

### What To Do If Compromised

**If You Suspect Your Account Was Hacked:**

1. **Stop**: Don't make any more transactions
2. **Change Password**: Use a different device if possible
3. **Check 2FA**: Verify your authenticator app settings
4. **Review History**: Look at recent transactions
5. **Contact Support**: Report compromise with details
6. **Monitor**: Watch account for 30 days afterward

**If You Lose Your Private Key:**

Unfortunately, lost private keys cannot be recovered. However:

1. You can still transfer tokens TO that wallet
2. That wallet will hold your tokens safely
3. Create a new wallet for future transactions
4. Transfer holdings once you have access to exchange or service that can help
